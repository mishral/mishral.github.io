Some Great Chicago Restaurants

Chicago has some of the best restaurnts. Below is a short list of some of my favorites, that I frequent often.

Mexican

  • El Habanero, Logan Square
  • L'Patron Tacos, Logan Square

Indian

  • Viceroy of Indian, Roger's Park
  • Rangoli, Bucktown

Thai

  • Thai Spice, Rogers Park
  • Sticky Rice, North Center

American

  • Sepia, West Loop
  • Bread and Wine, Irving Park

Recipe Bloggers I Love

A short list of the many food and recipe blogs I follow!

  • Smitten Kitchen: American classics made accessible
  • Skinny Taste: Popular recipes made healthy, with a Latin flair
  • Joy the Baker: Delicious pastries and baked goods
  • Indian Simmer: Collection of classic Indian recipes
  • Woks of Life: Chinese and Asian recipes made accessible
